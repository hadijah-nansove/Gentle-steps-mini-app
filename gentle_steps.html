🌿 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

When anxiety or panic rises, this technique gently brings you back to the present moment. Tap each step as you complete it. 💙

5

5 things you can SEE 👁️

Look around slowly. Name 5 things you can see right now — a colour, an object, a pattern on the wall.

4

4 things you can TOUCH 🤚

Feel the texture of 4 things around you — your clothes, a surface, the air, your own skin.

3

3 things you can HEAR 👂

Close your eyes and listen. Name 3 sounds — near or far, loud or very faint.

2

2 things you can SMELL 👃

Take a slow breath. What do you smell? Your skin, the air, nearby food or drink.

1

1 thing you can TASTE 👅

Notice what you can taste right now — even the faintest taste or just the sensation of your mouth.

🌿 Beautiful. You've returned to the present. Take a deep breath. You are safe. 💚

🔒 Your Privacy Promise

At Gentle Steps, your privacy is not just a policy — it is a promise. Here is exactly how we protect you.

🖥️ Everything Stays On Your Device

Your journal entries, mood logs, sleep data — all of it is stored only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is ever sent to any server. No account needed. No cloud. No data collection.

👁️ Nobody Can See Your Data

We have no backend, no database, no analytics. Absolutely zero visibility into what you write or how you feel. Your words are yours alone.

🚫 No Ads. No Tracking. No Selling.

Gentle Steps contains no advertisements, no trackers, and no third-party cookies. Your vulnerability will never be used to sell you something.

⬇️ Download Your Progress

Your data is yours. Download a copy of all your moods, journal entries, and sleep logs to keep forever — even if you clear your browser or switch devices. Your file never leaves your device.

🗑️ You Control Your Data

You can delete everything at any time. Your data, your decision — always.

💙 A Note From Hadijah

"I built Gentle Steps because I've been in that dark place too. I know how important it is to have somewhere safe to land. This app is my way of holding space for you — without judgment, without watching, without selling. You are safe here."
